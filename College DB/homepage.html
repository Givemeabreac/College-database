ABOUT US


AHIS is a college located in Chittagong, B. Our mission is to provide quality education to our students and prepare them for successful careers in their chosen fields. We offer a wide range of programs and courses, including business, engineering, and liberal arts.
At AHIS, we believe that education is not just about acquiring knowledge, but also about developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills. Our faculty consists of experienced professionals who are committed to providing students with a challenging and supportive learning environment. We also offer a range of extracurricular activities and opportunities for students to gain practical experience in their fields of study. We are proud of our students' achievements and look forward to helping more students achieve their goals.
